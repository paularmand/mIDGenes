mIDGenes - Measuring IDea GENeration EffectivenesS

This software was developed to support the development of metrics to evaluate an idea space, in turn this can be used to evaluate idea generation methods (like brainstorming, ...).
This software was developed by the Design Methodologies and Life Cycle Engineering group of the CIB - Centre for Industrial Management / Traffic and Infrastructure at the Katholieke University Leuven in Belgium.
